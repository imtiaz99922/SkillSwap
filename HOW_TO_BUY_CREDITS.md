## 🎯 Quick Overview

SkillSwap now supports purchasing credits using SSLCommerz payment gateway. Credits can be used to:

## 💳 Getting Started

### Step 1: Navigate to Buy Credits

1. Log in to your SkillSwap account
2. Click on **"Buy Credits"** in the navigation menu (or go to `/buy-credits`)

### Step 2: Choose Your Package

We offer 4 credit packages:

| Package           | Credits | Price   | Per Taka |
| ----------------- | ------- | ------- | -------- |
| **🌟 Starter**    | 100     | ৳500    | 0.20     |
| **💎 Pro**        | 500     | ৳2,000  | 0.25     |
| **🔥 Business**   | 1,500   | ৳5,000  | 0.30     |
| **⭐ Enterprise** | 5,000   | ৳15,000 | 0.33     |

_Pro tip: Enterprise package gives best value per taka!_

### Step 3: Make Payment

1. Click **"Buy Now"** on your chosen package
2. You'll be redirected to SSLCommerz payment gateway
3. Enter your payment details (card, mobile banking, etc.)
4. Confirm the payment

### Step 4: Success! ✅

- You'll be redirected back to SkillSwap
- Your credits will appear in your wallet instantly
- You can see the transaction in your payment history

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: How long does payment take?

**A**: Payment is usually confirmed within 1-2 seconds. Credits appear in your wallet immediately after confirmation.

### Q: Can I get a refund?

**A**: Contact support to discuss refund options. Standard refund policy applies.

### Q: Are my payment details safe?

**A**: Yes! We use SSLCommerz, a trusted payment gateway. Your card information is never stored on our servers - SSLCommerz handles all payment processing securely.

### Q: Can I change my package?

**A**: You can purchase any package at any time. Your credits are cumulative in your wallet.

### Q: Is there a limit on credits?

**A**: No! You can purchase as many credits as you want.

### Q: How do I check my purchase history?

**A**: Go to `/buy-credits` and scroll to "Recent Transactions" to see all your payments.

### Q: What if payment fails?

**A**: You won't be charged. Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, contact support.

### Q: Can I use the same card multiple times?

**A**: Yes! SSLCommerz allows repeated purchases with the same payment method.
